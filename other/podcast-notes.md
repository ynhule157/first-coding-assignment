# Additional Material: Podcast Notes
**Topic:** The Future of AI in Search
**Source:** Search Engine Journal Podcast

**Quick Thoughts:**
- AI overviews are pushing SEOs to focus more on brand building and direct traffic.
- Tools like Cursor and ChatGPT are becoming essential for technical SEO audits, not just content generation.
- The concept of "Information Gain" is more critical than ever to stand out from AI-generated summaries.
